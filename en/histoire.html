Our story

DEM — The brand
of movement

DEM is a Senegalese lifestyle brand that turns urban and regional identities into clothing, objects and visual experiences.

We believe African cities have powerful stories — but they are often poorly told.

DEM wants to change that.

Our first collection, DEM DAKAR, pays tribute to a city that never sleeps: a city of movement, noise, sea, heat, culture and ambition.

The origin

A brand born from a video.

What if Dakar were a brand?

That idea reached thousands of people. They started asking: "Can we actually wear this?" That's how DEM DAKAR was born — not as a simple collection, but as the first chapter of a movement.

Our mission

Turning our territories into strong, desirable, exportable brands.

Blue — the horizon

Think big, aim for export, tell Africa to the world.

Red — the strength

The intensity of the street, the energy of youth, the pride.

Green — balance

A brand rooted in its culture, looking to the future.

The movement continues

After Dakar,
all of Senegal.

Thiès, Saint-Louis, Casamance, and beyond: DEM will tell the other territories of Senegal and Africa. Each collection — a city, a region, an energy.
